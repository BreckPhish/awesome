Sorting rules
  • 3D Tips (Table 1): Bussers, Dishwashers, Prep Cooks, Cooks, and all Three Daughters employees, combined by first name; Three Daughters employees keep a 3D suffix and sort first.
  • Tin Plate (Table 2): Servers, Bartenders, Bar Lead, and Trainer — split into the AM pool (clock-in before 2:00 PM) and PM pool (2:00 PM & after). Trainee is excluded.
  • Always excluded: the ghost Three Daughters employee profile, Online ordering, Toast default, Hosts, Managers (incl. Hourly Shift Manager), and Chefs — never in any tip table.
  • Hours source: uses Payable Hours first, then falls back to Total Hours.
